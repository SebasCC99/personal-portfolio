Born in Venezuela, I moved to the US in 2019. Reading every time I could, I came across Ray Dalio and his book Principles. In it, Dalio talked about his approach to investing in the financial markets which sparked a fire in me to do the same!

After mentoring and online research, I started my engineering journey with Python for its begineer friendly caractheristic. With the desire to get professional experience under my belt as soon as possible, I attended Galvanize's Hack Reactor's Full Stack Bootcamp, from which I graduated in october of 2021.

At Net32 Inc since March 2023, as a Full Stack Engineer, I've led the enhancement of user interfaces and system performance through dynamic Next.js features and the migration of React pages. My work with Product Owners on a vendor performance dashboard significantly streamlined data access and improved the frontend experience for U.S. dentists. Focusing on backend improvements, I developed RESTful APIs and a Java backend using Spring Boot, ensuring the application's security and performance.

As a Junior Front-end Engineer at FTD from January 2022 to February 2023, I enhanced our eCommerce UI, directly impacting sales and revenue through scalable software solutions. My responsibilities included developing Storybook components, writing Jest tests in line with Test-Driven Development practices, and managing A/B testing to optimize performance. This role allowed me to effectively address core business needs through close collaboration with Product Owners and stakeholders.

In my role as a Software Engineer Immersive Resident at Galvanize Inc from October 2021 to January 2022, I mentored over 30 students in a comprehensive software engineering program focused on JavaScript. I guided them through complex concepts and debugging techniques, emphasizing coding best practices and Agile methodologies. My contribution was pivotal in fostering a comprehensive understanding of software development among students.
